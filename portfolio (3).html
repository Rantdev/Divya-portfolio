Hello! I'm divya — a UI/UX Designer with a deep passion for creating interfaces that feel as good as they look. I believe great design is invisible: when it works perfectly, users don't notice it — they just feel at ease.

As a first-year B.Tech student, I'm deeply interested in design and the art of crafting elegant, user-centered experiences. I'm constantly learning, experimenting, and improving my design thinking
